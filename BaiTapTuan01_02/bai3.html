Consequently, he becomes acquainted with the inner city stress, and realizes the harsh existence of the homeless, and vows to give them a chance in the courts. The Street Lawyer is a great book. It is well written and interesting. Other books by John Grisham include The Film,The Pelican Brief, and The Cilent.
